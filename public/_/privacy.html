Privacy

CloudNote D1 is designed as a zero-knowledge note app: content is encrypted in your browser and stored as ciphertext in D1.

What we store

  • Slug (the URL path) as the note identifier
  • Encrypted content (Base64 ciphertext)
  • Encryption parameters (IV + salt, Base64)
  • Timestamps and size metadata
  • Version history (last 10 encrypted snapshots)

What we do not store

  • Your password
  • Plaintext note content

Important note

If someone learns your slug, they can download the ciphertext. Use a strong password to resist offline guessing.
